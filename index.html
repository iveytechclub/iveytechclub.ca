About Us

Ivey Tech Club is a student-run organization at Ivey Business School that fosters a community of individuals interested in tech. We share opportunities, discuss tech topics, and support each other's tech projects, helping kickstart careers in tech. Our mission is to make tech a more viable and prominent potential career path for Ivey students.

What We Provide

Events

From guest speakers, panels, office tours, socials, workshops, and much more, we engage our members with a variety of activities.

Resources

Members can learn about a variety of resources from book recommendations, podcasts, newsletters, case prep, and other online resources.

Mentorship

Upper-year mentors share their knowledge and experience for their mentees and other ITC members to learn from.

Network

Join a network of successful and like-minded peers for support and collaboration on projects and recruiting.
